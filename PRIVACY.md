# Privacy Policy for Hafez Oracle

Last updated: 2026-05-09

Summary
-------
Hafez Oracle is an offline Android app that provides poetic/fortune-style readings inspired by Hafez. The app runs entirely on the user's device and is designed to be private and minimal.

What this policy covers
-----------------------
This policy explains what (if any) data the app collects and how it is used.

No internet, no remote services
------------------------------
- The app does not require an internet connection to operate. All content and features work locally on your device.
- The app does not contact servers, and it does not upload or download data.

No ads, analytics, accounts, or cloud sync
------------------------------------------
- The app contains no advertising.
- The app does not include analytics, tracking libraries, or telemetry.
- The app does not require or use user accounts.
- The app offers no cloud backup or sync features.

No personal data collection
---------------------------
- The app does not collect, send, or store personal data about users.
- The app does not collect device identifiers, location, contacts, or usage metrics.
- Any settings or preferences you set are stored only on your device (locally) and are not transmitted anywhere.

Permissions
-----------
- The app only requests permissions necessary for offline operation (if any). If the app requests no runtime permissions, none are required.
- If a specific permission (e.g., storage) is required, it will be requested at runtime and used only for its stated purpose; no data is sent off-device.

Security and storage
--------------------
- Any data stored by the app (for example, user-selected preferences) is kept locally on your device.
- The project owner does not maintain any server-side storage or backups for user data.

Third-party services or libraries
---------------------------------
- The app does not include third-party analytics or ad services.
- If future versions add third-party libraries, they will be documented here.

Children
--------
- The app is not intended specifically for children. Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no targeted data collection for children.

Changes to this policy
----------------------
This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will indicate the most recent change.
