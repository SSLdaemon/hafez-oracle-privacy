Privacy Policy — Hafez Oracle

Last updated: 2026-05-09

Overview

Hafez Oracle is an offline Android app that provides poetic, reflective readings inspired by the poetry of Hafez. The app is designed with privacy and simplicity in mind.

No internet or remote services

The app works entirely on your device. It does not contact any servers, and it does not use the internet for its core features.

No ads, analytics, accounts, or cloud sync

There are no advertisements, no analytics/telemetry, no user accounts, and no cloud synchronization.

No personal data collection

The app does not collect or transmit personal data. Any preferences you set (e.g., theme or font size) are stored locally on your device only.

Permissions

The app requests only permissions necessary for local operation (if any). It does not request permissions for analytics, tracking, or remote storage.
